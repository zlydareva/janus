FOR WHOM

Janus is independent magazine where we collect critical opinions on the topics of people, value, support and inspiration in the realm of art and money.We are driven by the society and searching articles that will bring you important information and provide with knowledge. We listen opinions of artists, critiques, collectors, and people who is related to the area of art and money.

WHAT FOR

On daily basis we pass through our fingers various banknotes and coins. We go to the museums and galleries and admire these paintings. But we never make clear connection between art, money, and us.

In Janus we intend to build a strong bridge between these elements and support all aspects related to the artistic sphere.

OUR VALUES

We believe that people and their feelings are in the center of art. Money is a media to measure our feelings.

We apply the prism of humanity to look at our relationship with art and money to help readers to question him/herself.
